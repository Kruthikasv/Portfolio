Project Overview

Skyalytics is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze extensive domestic flight data from major US airlines. The project examines on-time performance, delays, cancellations, and diversions to uncover hidden patterns and trends in air travel efficiency.

The dashboard enables users to identify recurring issues, understand cancellation reasons, and recognize overall performance trends. This analysis benefits stakeholders including airlines seeking operational improvements, travelers planning trips, and policymakers developing transportation regulations.

Key Insights

What the Data Revealed

  • Daily impact: An average of 36,712 flights experienced delays each day, affecting millions of passengers
  • Cancellation patterns: Approximately 720 flights canceled daily by US carriers in 2019
  • Weather dominance: Weather-related issues emerged as the leading cause of flight disruptions
  • Seasonal trends: Clear patterns in delay frequency correlating with holiday travel periods
  • Airline variability: Significant performance differences between carriers on similar routes

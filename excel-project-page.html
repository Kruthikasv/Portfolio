Key Insights

What the Data Revealed

  • Mid/late-life risk spike: Comorbid diabetes and hypertension rises steadily with age, peaking at 30%+ in the 60-69 and 80-90 age groups
  • Cancer by sex: Near-identical cancer prevalence (12% males vs 13% females) indicates sex is not a major differentiator
  • BMI-health gradient: Normal BMI correlates with Excellent/Very Good ratings, while Obese skews to Fair/Poor
  • Health declines with age: Clear shift from Excellent/Very Good in younger groups toward Fair/Poor in 70+ brackets

Project Overview

This Tableau Story investigates the global impact of renewable energy adoption on carbon dioxide emissions. The analysis spans multiple dimensions including geographic distribution of emissions, categorical breakdowns by sector, and individual carbon footprint patterns.

The project aims to visualize the relationship between countries' renewable energy investments and their corresponding emission trajectories, providing data-driven insights for environmental policy discussions.

Key Analysis Areas

What the Dashboard Explores

  • CO2 Worldwide: Geographic visualization of carbon emissions across countries and regions
  • CO2 Categories: Breakdown of emissions by sector (transportation, industry, energy, agriculture)
  • CO2 Footprint: Individual and per-capita carbon footprint analysis across demographics
  • Renewable Impact: Correlation between renewable energy adoption rates and emission changes
  • Trend Analysis: Historical patterns and projections for global emissions
